Bank Marketing Analytics Dashboard

📊 Project Overview

The Bank Marketing Analytics Dashboard is a data analytics project designed to analyze bank marketing campaign performance, customer behavior, and subscription trends. The dashboard provides meaningful insights that help understand customer engagement and the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. This project focuses on transforming raw banking marketing data into interactive and insightful visualizations using Microsoft Power BI.

🎯 Problem Statement

Marketing campaigns play a crucial role in attracting new customers and promoting financial products. However, understanding which strategies and communication channels perform best can be challenging. The objective of this project is to analyze marketing campaign data to identify patterns in customer responses, communication channels, and demographic segments that influence subscription outcomes.

📁 Dataset Information

The dataset contains customer-related marketing campaign information including:

Customer Age

Job Type

Education Level

Contact Communication Type

Previous Campaign Contacts

Call Duration

Account Balance

Month of Contact

Subscription Status (Yes / No)

📌 Dashboard KPIs

The dashboard highlights key performance indicators such as:

Total Campaign Attempts: 125K

Total Customers: 45.21K

Average Account Balance: 1.36K

These KPIs provide a quick overview of campaign scale and customer financial characteristics.

📈 Key Insights Customers with secondary education represent the largest customer segment.

Cellular communication generates significantly higher campaign interactions compared to telephone.

Customer subscriptions vary across different job categories.

Monthly trends reveal fluctuations in marketing campaign engagement.

The relationship between previous contacts and call duration indicates customer interaction levels.

📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes several interactive visualizations:

Customers by Education Level (Donut Chart)

Previous Contacts vs Call Duration (Scatter Plot)

Subscriptions by Job (Bar Chart)

Subscriptions by Month (Line Chart)

Marketing Campaign Conversion Funnel

KPI Cards for Campaign Overview

Customer Segmentation Filter
